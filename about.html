Our Story

Who We Are

Nile Petroleum Corporation (Nilepet) is the National Oil Company of the Republic of South Sudan. Established to manage the nation’s vast petroleum resources, Nilepet is entrusted with the exploration, production, refining, and marketing of oil and gas across the country.

Since our founding, we have grown into a leading energy enterprise with a presence in every facet of the petroleum value chain. Our operations span upstream exploration in South Sudan’s most promising basins, downstream refining and distribution, and strategic joint ventures with global energy partners.

At Nilepet, we are guided by a singular purpose: to harness South Sudan’s energy wealth for the sustainable development of our nation and the well-being of our people. We are committed to transparency, operational excellence, and the highest standards of environmental and social responsibility.

Our Journey

Our History

Milestones in Nilepet’s journey to become a leading national oil company.

2005

Founding of Nilepet

Nile Petroleum Corporation is established as the National Oil Company of South Sudan following the Comprehensive Peace Agreement.

2011

Independence & Expansion

South Sudan gains independence. Nilepet takes full ownership of national petroleum assets and begins expanding operations across the country.

2015

Joint Ventures Launched

Strategic partnerships established with international energy majors to accelerate exploration and production in key oil blocks.

2020

Refinery & Downstream Growth

Nilepet commissions new refining capacity and expands its retail network across South Sudan, enhancing domestic fuel security.

2024

Gas & Energy Transition

Nilepet launches its gas development and power generation strategy, supporting South Sudan’s energy transition and industrialization agenda.

2026

New Exploration Frontiers

Nilepet announces major new exploration blocks and continues to drive innovation and sustainable development across the energy value chain.
