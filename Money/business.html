Upstream Exploration & Production

Nilepet is at the forefront of hydrocarbon exploration and production in South Sudan. Our upstream operations span the country’s most prolific basins, where we leverage advanced seismic technology and drilling techniques to unlock new reserves.

We are committed to maximizing recovery rates while maintaining the highest standards of safety, environmental stewardship, and community engagement.

  • Exploration & seismic data acquisition
  • Drilling and well completion
  • Production optimization & reservoir management
  • Health, safety & environmental (HSE) excellence

Downstream Refining & Distribution

Nilepet’s downstream operations ensure that petroleum products reach every corner of South Sudan. We operate modern refining facilities, strategic storage depots, and an expanding network of retail stations.

Our commitment to quality and reliability ensures that businesses, industries, and households have access to the fuel they need to thrive.

  • Crude oil refining & processing
  • Product storage & logistics
  • Retail network expansion (50+ stations)

Gas Development & Power

Natural gas is a cornerstone of South Sudan’s energy future. Nilepet is actively developing gas resources to support power generation, industrial growth, and household energy needs.

Our gas strategy aligns with the nation’s energy transition goals, providing cleaner, more efficient energy solutions while reducing environmental impact.

  • Gas exploration & production
  • Power generation projects
  • Industrial gas supply
