Who We Are

Building progress through responsible operations

Nile Petroleum Corporation, commonly known as NILEPET, is the national oil and gas company of the Republic of South Sudan. It represents the government's commercial interests in the country's petroleum industry and participates in upstream, midstream, and downstream oil and gas operations.

NILEPET was established in 2003 under the Civil Authority for New Sudan (CANS) during the liberation period. It was formally incorporated on 1 June 2009 as a state-owned company. Following South Sudan's independence in 2011, it became the country's national oil company. Today, its operations are governed by the National Petroleum and Gas Corporation (NILEPET) Act, 2019.

Business Overview

Creating value across the petroleum industry

Nile Petroleum Corporation Limited is the state-owned national oil and gas company of the Republic of South Sudan. Its mandate includes participating in upstream, midstream and downstream petroleum activities on behalf of the government.

The Corporation represents the national interest in petroleum ventures and works with local and international partners to support exploration, production, transportation, storage, distribution and marketing of petroleum products.

  • Participation in petroleum exploration, development and production.
  • Development of downstream petroleum infrastructure and markets.
  • Management of national interests in joint operating companies and ventures.
  • Promotion of safety, environmental protection, local content and workforce development.
