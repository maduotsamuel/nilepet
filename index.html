A Word from Our Managing Director

Welcome to Nile Petroleum Corporation. As the National Oil Company of South Sudan, we are driven by a clear mission: to responsibly develop our nation's petroleum resources for the benefit of all South Sudanese.

Our commitment extends beyond energy production; we are dedicated to fostering sustainable growth, building local capacity, and creating lasting value for our communities and partners. We believe in transparency, operational excellence, and the highest standards of environmental and social responsibility.

I invite you to explore our website and learn more about how we are powering South Sudan's energy future. Together, we can build a prosperous and sustainable future for our nation.

Who We Are

About Nilepet

Mandated to manage and develop South Sudan’s petroleum resources for the benefit of the people. Nile Petroleum Corporation (Nilepet) is the state-owned enterprise responsible for the exploration, production, refining, and marketing of oil and gas across the nation.

With a commitment to transparency, sustainability, and national development, Nilepet partners with international energy leaders to unlock the full potential of South Sudan’s energy sector.
